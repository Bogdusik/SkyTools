# Privacy Policy - SkyTools

## Location Data Usage

**Important:** SkyTools does NOT access or use your device's location.

### What We Do:
- ✅ Read drone position data from DJI SDK (aircraft location)
- ✅ Store flight telemetry data locally on your device
- ✅ Display flight paths on maps using stored drone coordinates

### What We DON'T Do:
- ❌ Access your device's GPS location via CLLocationManager
- ❌ Request location permissions from iOS
- ❌ Transmit any location data to DJI or third-party servers
- ❌ Share your personal location or flight data externally

### Data Storage:
- All flight data (telemetry, GPS coordinates, events) is stored **locally** on your device
- Data is saved in the app's Documents directory
- You can export data manually via Share Sheet (JSON, CSV, GPX)
- No automatic cloud sync or data transmission

### DJI SDK:
- SkyTools uses DJI Mobile SDK to communicate with your drone
- We only **read** telemetry data from the SDK
- We do **not** send your location or personal data to DJI
- DJI SDK may have its own privacy policies - please review DJI's terms

## Your Rights:
- You control all exported data
- You can delete flight sessions at any time
- No data is collected without your explicit action (export/share)
